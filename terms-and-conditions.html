2 Description of the App

Roles Witch AI is a voice assistant application that enables you to interact with artificial intelligence models through text, voice, and vision. The App supports two modes of operation:

  • Local mode: AI inference performed entirely on your device using on-device models.
  • Remote mode: AI inference performed by a third-party server that you configure within the App. The App merely acts as a client that transmits your inputs to the endpoint you specify.

What is "inference"? In the context of these Terms, "inference" means the act of running an already-trained AI model to produce an output (such as a text reply, a transcription, or an analysis of an image) from an input you provide. It is the step that actually generates the AI's response. Inference can happen locally — entirely on your iPhone's hardware with no network activity — or remotely, on a third-party server you have chosen to connect to, in which case your input is transmitted to that server over the internet and the server's response is sent back to the App. Roles Witch AI does not train AI models and does not operate inference servers of its own; it only performs local inference on your device or forwards your input to the remote endpoint you configured.
