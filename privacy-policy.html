§ Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Roles Witch AI (the "App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the App on iOS. Roles Witch AI is a free application that functions as a client for local, on-device AI models as well as any third-party AI server you choose to configure.

We have designed Roles Witch AI to minimize data collection to the greatest extent reasonably possible. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please do not use the App.

In short: We do not collect analytics. We do not track you. We do not operate servers that receive your conversations. Your data stays on your device unless you personally choose to connect the App to a remote AI server.

1 Data Collection Policy

We do not collect, receive, store, or process any personal information about you. Roles Witch AI does not include analytics SDKs, telemetry frameworks, crash reporting services, advertising identifiers, fingerprinting, or any other tracking technology.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information
  • Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising IDs)
  • Your IP address or approximate location
  • Your conversations, prompts, voice recordings, or any messages you exchange with AI models
  • Usage statistics, feature interaction logs, or behavioral analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information

The App operates without user accounts. You do not register, log in, or create a profile to use Roles Witch AI.

2 App Store and Device Permissions

When you download the App from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect certain information in accordance with Apple's own privacy policy, which is independent of ours. We do not control, receive, or have access to any information Apple collects during installation, purchase, or delivery of the App.

The App may request the following device permissions, which are processed locally on your device:

  • Microphone: Required to enable voice input for conversations with AI models. Raw audio is never transmitted to us. Audio may, however, be processed by Apple's Speech Recognition service as described below.
  • Camera: Required only if you choose to use the vision or video call features. Images and video frames are processed on your device or, if you have configured a remote AI server, sent to that server at your direction.
  • Speech Recognition: The App uses Apple's Speech Recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer) to transcribe your voice into text. Depending on your device, language, and iOS capabilities, Apple may perform this transcription either on your device or on Apple's servers. When server-based recognition is used, short audio clips are sent to Apple and handled under Apple's own privacy policy — not ours. You can enforce on-device transcription directly within the App by enabling "Require on-device recognition" in the App's Settings; when this option is enabled, the App instructs the system to perform speech recognition exclusively on your device, and no audio is sent to Apple. We never receive the audio or the resulting transcript beyond what is needed to pass the text to your selected AI model (local or remote, per your configuration).
  • Network Access: Used solely when you explicitly configure the App to communicate with a remote AI server of your choice.

You may revoke any of these permissions at any time through your iOS Settings. Revoking a permission will disable the associated feature but will not otherwise affect the App.

No Siri or Shortcuts Integration: Roles Witch AI does not integrate with Siri, SiriKit, App Intents, or the Shortcuts app. We do not expose any voice commands, intents, or automations to the operating system.

3 Local Data Storage

All data generated through your use of Roles Witch AI is stored exclusively on your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • App settings, preferences, and configuration
  • Characters, personas, and custom roles you create
  • Conversation history and chat transcripts
  • Any AI models you download for on-device inference
  • Remote server endpoints, API keys, and credentials you enter to connect to third-party AI services

This information remains under your sole control at all times. It is never copied, synchronized, or transmitted to any server operated by us. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed by iOS, subject to any backups you have independently configured (for example, through iCloud Backup or device-level backups).

Credentials you provide: Any API keys, tokens, or credentials you enter to connect to a remote AI service are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. We never transmit, receive, or have visibility into these credentials.

4 On-Device AI Models

Roles Witch AI supports running AI models directly on your device. When you use an on-device model:

  • The model runs entirely offline within the App
  • Your prompts, inputs, and the model's responses never leave your device
  • No data is transmitted to us or to any third party
  • Network connectivity is not required for inference

On-device models may be downloaded within the App. The download itself is a standard HTTPS request to the model's hosting location and is subject to that host's own terms and privacy practices. Once downloaded, the model is stored locally and used without further network access.

5 Remote AI Servers and Third-Party Services

Roles Witch AI can be configured to connect to any AI server or API endpoint that you choose, including self-hosted models, OpenAI-compatible services, and other third-party inference providers. This functionality is entirely optional and is never enabled by default without your explicit configuration.

When you configure and use a remote AI server, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • The App acts solely as a client that transmits the data you provide (prompts, messages, images, audio, and related metadata) to the server endpoint you specified.
  • The remote server is operated by a third party that is independent of us. Roles Witch AI and its developers are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or responsible for any remote AI service, model provider, or inference host.
  • The remote service's own terms of service, acceptable use policy, and privacy policy govern how your data is handled once it leaves your device. You are solely responsible for reviewing, understanding, and accepting those terms before connecting to the service.
  • We have no control over and no visibility into how the remote server stores, processes, retains, trains on, shares, or deletes your data.
  • The choice of which remote server to use — and the decision to transmit any data to it — is entirely yours. By configuring a remote endpoint, you expressly authorize the App to communicate with it on your behalf.

Important: If you are concerned about data privacy, we strongly recommend using on-device models exclusively, or only connecting to remote servers operated by parties you trust and whose privacy terms you have reviewed. Data transmitted to third-party servers is governed entirely by that third party's policies and is outside our control.

6 No Analytics, No Tracking, No Advertising

Roles Witch AI contains no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Sentry, Crashlytics, AppsFlyer, Adjust, or any comparable third-party SDK.

We do not build user profiles. We do not measure engagement. We do not A/B test. We do not attribute installs. We do not fingerprint devices. We do not monetize attention.

Because we do not collect analytics, there is nothing for you to opt out of — privacy is the default and only option.

7 Children's Privacy

Roles Witch AI is not directed to, marketed to, or intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided information to a remote AI service through the App, please contact the operator of that remote service directly, as such data is not accessible to us.

Parents and guardians are responsible for supervising the use of the App by minors, including reviewing the terms and privacy practices of any remote AI server their child may configure.

8 Your Responsibilities

Because Roles Witch AI stores data locally and does not maintain servers on your behalf, you are responsible for:

  • Securing your device with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID to prevent unauthorized access to locally stored data.
  • Managing your backups. If you enable iCloud Backup or device backups, locally stored data may be included; review Apple's backup settings to control this.
  • Reviewing third-party terms. Before connecting the App to any remote AI server, read and accept that provider's terms of service and privacy policy.
  • Safeguarding your credentials. API keys and tokens you enter are stored on your device; do not share screenshots or device backups that may expose them.
  • Appropriate use. You are solely responsible for the content you generate, the prompts you submit to remote services, and compliance with the laws and terms applicable to your use.

Roles Witch AI enables interactions with artificial intelligence models that generate text, voice, and other content. AI-generated output may contain inaccuracies, errors, biases, or content that is offensive, inappropriate, or otherwise objectionable. Such output does not represent our views or opinions.

You should independently verify any information that is material to your decisions. The App is provided for personal, informational, and entertainment purposes only and is not a substitute for professional advice (medical, legal, financial, or otherwise).

10 International Users and Applicable Law

Roles Witch AI is available globally through the Apple App Store. Because we do not collect personal data, most international data-protection obligations (including the GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and similar regimes) are inapplicable to us as a data controller or processor. If you configure the App to connect to a remote AI server, the operator of that server may be subject to such regulations, and you should consult that operator's privacy policy.

You are responsible for ensuring that your use of the App and any remote services complies with the laws of your jurisdiction.
